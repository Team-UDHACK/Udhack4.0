About the Hackathon

UDHACK 4.0 is the annual inter-college hackathon where the brightest minds come together to innovate, build, and compete. Join us for a thrilling journey of creativity, collaboration, and coding.

Problem Statements

1. Smart Campus Solutions

Innovate ways to make campus life smarter and more efficient using technology.

2. Healthcare Tech

Build solutions to improve healthcare accessibility, diagnosis, or management.

3. Sustainability & Environment

Develop ideas to promote sustainability and protect the environment.

4. FinTech for Students

Create tools to help students manage finances, scholarships, or expenses.

5. Social Impact

Design solutions that address social issues and empower communities.

Any innovative idea that solves a real-world problem. Surprise us!
